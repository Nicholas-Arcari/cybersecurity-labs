WiFi (The Basics)

This is the largest subfolder. Here you store scripts for capturing WPA Handshakes and for creating rogue access points.

Must-Have Tools: Aircrack-ng (essential suite), Wifite (to automate the entire process), Kismet (to detect hidden networks).

Key Concept: This is also where you keep WiFi-specific Wordlists (often numeric-only or 8-character long).
