SDR (Radio Frequency)

This is the most advanced section. If you have an antenna (such as an RTL-SDR for around 30 EUR or a HackRF), this is where you save the radio signals you capture (gate openers, car remotes, weather stations).

Must-Have Tools: Universal Radio Hacker (URH) (excellent for visualizing and modifying a recorded radio signal), GQRX (for listening to radio).
